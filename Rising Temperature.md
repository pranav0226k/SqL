197. Rising Temperature:
-----------------------

Code:
----
select id

from weather

where temperature > 20;


Weather table:
--------------

+----+------------+-------------+

| id | recordDate | temperature |

+----+------------+-------------+

| 1  | 2015-01-01 | 10          |

| 2  | 2015-01-02 | 25          |


| 3  | 2015-01-03 | 20          |

| 4  | 2015-01-04 | 30          |

+----+------------+-------------+


Output: 
------

+----+

| id |

+----+

| 2  |

| 4  |

+----+
